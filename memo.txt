<디버그>
gdb ./bin/a.out  
run < input  

b <line_num>: break point  
n: step over (next line)  
s: step into  
fin: step out  
c: coninue  
p <expr>: print evaluated expression  
l: show next 10 line  
bt: show callstack (backtrace)  
q: quit
